Welcome to Lake Miramar Golf Course

Lake Mira Mar Golf Course is an 9-hole golf course set in the hill of Mira Mar Lake. It is surrounded by 9,000 acres of hills with a running path visited by many for jogging, picnic, and for enjoying the view.

It over looks Mira Mesa to its west and Poway conveniently to its east, this course hase been rated as one of the best golf course in central San Diego.

Convinient Spot for the family!

Lake Mira Mar Golf Course is an affordable escape from the city. Enjoy the fresh air with your loved ones or simply meet new friends.We have a snack bar conviniently located right next to the parking lot along with bikes which are availble for rent.Take advantage of the boats we have on the docks and have a nice tour around the lake.

You are sure to enjoy this beautiful location.

Choose an Adventrure


  • Picnic for everyone

    Enjoy a beautiful day at the lake by bringing family, friends, and food! With over 10 spots for picnic and a breath taking view, you are sure to enjoy! Grills are also available for use through out the lake and a snack bar conviniently located by the entrance.

  • Lake Adventures

    Bring your own boat or rent one at our Concesssion Stand! There is a dock access located by the Proshop. Here you can safely dock your boat or kayak and set off into the lake. Please advice of the ducks. They are a handful. If you need assistant please come by the Concession stand! We are more than happily to provide the best experience for you.

  • 5K Path

    There is a path that goes around the golf course and the lake. If you are here to simply enjoy a walk or put in some miles into your daily exercise then this is the spot to be! The path is 5 miles long with small hiking trails and shortcuts. Pets are allowed!
